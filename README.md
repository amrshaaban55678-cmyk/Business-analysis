## Project Name

Healthcare Analytics

## Project Idea

The core objective is to transform raw healthcare data into "Actionable Insights." The project focuses on three pillars found in your dataset:
Operational Efficiency: Analyzing waiting times, appointment delays, and bed occupancy rates to identify bottlenecks in the patient journey.
Patient Experience (CX): Examining the relationship between staff/doctor ratings, hospital cleanliness, and the Net Promoter Score (NPS) to understand what drives patient loyalty.
Financial & Clinical Outcomes: Correlating treatment costs and insurance types with medical success rates and patient readmission risks to ensure high-quality care is also sustainable.

## Project Plan

### Responsibilities:

Phase 1: Data Cleaning & Pre-processing

Handle missing values in critical columns like Doctor_Rating and Emergency_Response_Time.

Standardize branch names and format date columns for time-series analysis.

Categorize numeric variables (e.g., grouping ages into bands like "Pediatric," "Adult," "Senior").

Phase 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Delay Analysis: Identify which departments (e.g., Oncology, Cardiology) suffer from the highest appointment delays.

Workload vs. Quality: Analyze if a high "Doctor Workload" directly leads to lower patient satisfaction ratings.

Channel Efficiency: Compare patient satisfaction across different visit channels (Walk-in vs. Mobile App vs. Website).

Phase 3: Financial & Risk Analysis

Profitability Mapping: Calculate Profit_per_patient across different insurance types (Private, Corporate, Self-Pay).

Risk Identification: Use the Service_Recovery_Risk and Complaint_Severity columns to identify "at-risk" patients who require immediate follow-up.

Phase 4: Dashboard Development

Build an interactive dashboard (using Power BI, Tableau, or Python/Streamlit) featuring:

KPI Overview: Average Wait Time, NPS Score, and Bed Occupancy Rate.

Trend Analysis: Tracking patient volume and satisfaction over months/days of the week.

Departmental Comparison: A breakdown of ratings and costs by department.

Phase 5: Recommendations & Final Report

Provide data-backed solutions to reduce wait times during peak hours.

Suggest a "Service Recovery" strategy for patients flagged as high-risk.

Highlight the most profitable and efficient hospital branches to serve as models for others.
